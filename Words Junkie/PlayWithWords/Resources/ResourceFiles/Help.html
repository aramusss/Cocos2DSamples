About

Words Junkie is a unique social word game in which players form words by placing one character in every turn. The players accumulate points based on the length of the word they formed. The game continues till a player resigns or there are no more empty tiles left on the board.

Features

Make friends by playing with random junkies like you.

Check out top junkies amongst friends and overall in the leader boards.

Share screenshots of the game board with your friends.

Opponent Selection

You can start a game with either your Facebook friends or a random opponent.

Game Play

Place a character in any empty tile on the board.

Form a word by selecting a sequence of characters that must include the character you just placed.

To select a sequence, tap on its characters.

The sequence can be either horizontal (left to right) or vertical (top to bottom).

Once a a word is selected, you tap on the play button to submit your word.

If you do not wish to place a character in your turn, you can pass.

You get 60 seconds to submit your move. If the time expires, your turn will be passed.

Rules

Points for each word formed are awarded by squaring the length of the word.

For example playing a two letter word like DO will give you 4 points and playing a 3 letter word like DON will give you 9 points.

Points will only be awarded for a dictionary word.

Points will not be rewarded for a word which has already been played.

You can not pass in two consecutive turns. Doing so will result in forfeiting the game to your opponent.
